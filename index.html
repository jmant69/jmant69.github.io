Introduction to Pure Lindy

Pure Lindy is the next step in Sian and Julian's Lindy Hop adventure.

Working predominantly in Manchester and the North West we offer private Lindy Hop tuition sessions where we can help you develop great lead and follow technique to help you dance confidently on the social dance floor with any partner.

If you are interested in learning or improving your Lindy Hop dancing please contact us and we'll set up a meeting to a discuss your needs.

We can analyse your current level and devise a personalised class or programme of classes tailored to your specific requirements.

We also offer the following:

  • corporate packages for team building
  • performances and demonstrations
  • learning packages suitable for school or colleges targeting curriculum objectives

Please take a look around the various pages on our site. They're not all as fully populated as we eventually intend them to be but we couldn't wait to get the site up and running as soon as possible so please bear with us.
